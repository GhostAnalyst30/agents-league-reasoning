# Assessment and Readiness Policy (Synthetic)

> SYNTHETIC DOCUMENT - Fabricated assessment policy for a fictional organization.

## Question Generation Rules

1. Every practice question must be grounded in approved knowledge sources and include a citation to the source section.
2. Questions must map to a specific skill of the target certification (see the certification skill list).
3. Each assessment covers at least 3 distinct skills with 2 questions per skill.
4. Difficulty mix: 40% foundational, 40% applied scenario, 20% advanced.

## Readiness Scoring

- Practice score is the rolling average of the last 3 assessments.
- **Exam-ready**: practice average >= 75% (Associate) or >= 78% (Expert) AND study hours >= 80% of the recommended hours.
- **Almost ready**: within 10 points of threshold — system schedules targeted review of the weakest skills.
- **Not ready**: more than 10 points below threshold — system loops back to the study plan and re-sequences weak areas first.

## Feedback Loop

After every assessment the system must:
1. Identify the 2 weakest skills.
2. Ask the Study Plan Generator to re-prioritize those skills in the next study window.
3. Notify the Engagement Agent to adjust checkpoint reminders.
4. Update the manager-level readiness aggregate (team view only, no raw individual answers).
